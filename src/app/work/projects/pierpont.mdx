## Overview

Pierpont is a specialized schedule management application designed for HORECA (Hotel, Restaurant, Cafe) operations. The platform provides a comprehensive solution for managing hotel guest services, coordinating golf events, scheduling breakfast services, and organizing various hospitality activities. Built to handle complex scheduling needs in the hospitality industry, Pierpont ensures seamless coordination of daily operations and special events.

## Key Features

- **Daily Schedule Management**: Comprehensive calendar system for managing daily activities including breakfast services, hotel guest accommodations, and special events.

- **Event Coordination**: Specialized tools for organizing and scheduling golf events, ensuring proper coordination between multiple activities and services.

- **Guest Service Scheduling**: Streamlined system for managing hotel guest services, breakfast times, and accommodation-related activities.

- **Multi-Activity Tracking**: Ability to manage multiple concurrent activities including breakfast services, golf events, and hotel guest needs simultaneously.

- **Intuitive Calendar Interface**: User-friendly calendar view that displays all scheduled activities in an easy-to-navigate format.

- **Real-Time Updates**: Dynamic scheduling system that allows for real-time updates and modifications to accommodate changing hospitality needs.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: React, Next.js, TypeScript
- **Backend**: Node.js
- **Database**: Modern database solution for schedule persistence
- **Deployment**: Vercel
- **UI/UX**: Modern responsive design for hospitality staff use

## Challenges and Learnings

Building a schedule management system for HORECA operations required understanding the unique needs of the hospitality industry, where multiple activities often occur simultaneously and require careful coordination. Key challenges included creating an intuitive interface that could handle complex scheduling scenarios while remaining simple enough for staff to use efficiently. The project highlighted the importance of designing for real-world operational needs in service industries.

## Outcome

Pierpont successfully delivers a robust scheduling solution that streamlines HORECA operations. The platform enables hospitality staff to efficiently manage guest services, events, and daily activities from a single, unified interface. By automating schedule coordination and providing clear visibility into all operations, Pierpont helps hospitality businesses improve operational efficiency and enhance guest experiences.

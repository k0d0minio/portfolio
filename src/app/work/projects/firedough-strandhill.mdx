## Overview

Firedough Pizza Strandhill is a mobile pizzeria that brings authentic Italian pizza to Ireland's west coast. Rooted in family recipes from Naples, the business combines traditional Neapolitan techniques with fresh local Irish ingredients. The mobile kitchen allows Firedough to serve authentic wood-fired pizzas at festivals, events, and private gatherings throughout the coastal region, creating a unique culinary experience that bridges Italian tradition with Irish hospitality.

## Key Features

- **Authentic Neapolitan Pizza**: Traditional recipes passed down through generations, using imported Italian flour and San Marzano tomatoes for authentic flavor.

- **Mobile Kitchen**: Fully equipped mobile pizzeria that can travel to festivals, events, and private gatherings, bringing authentic pizza directly to customers.

- **Local Ingredient Integration**: Combines traditional Italian techniques with fresh Irish ingredients, including local salmon, mushrooms, and farmhouse cheeses.

- **Signature Menu**: Curated selection of pizzas including Margherita Classica, Atlantic Salmon, Wild Mushroom & Truffle, Coastal Pepperoni, Irish Farmhouse, and Quattro Stagioni.

- **Event Catering**: Specialized private event catering services, bringing the authentic pizza experience to weddings, corporate events, and celebrations.

- **Community Engagement**: Active participation in local festivals and events, building connections with the coastal community and supporting local farms and producers.

- **Wood-Fired Cooking**: Traditional wood-fired oven ensures authentic Neapolitan pizza with perfect char and flavor.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: Modern web framework for showcasing menu, events, and contact information
- **E-commerce**: Online ordering and booking system for events
- **Content Management**: Easy-to-update system for menu items and event schedules
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-optimized for customers on the go
- **Social Integration**: Integration with social media for event promotion and community engagement

## Challenges and Learnings

Building a website for a mobile food business required understanding the unique needs of a service that operates at multiple locations. Key challenges included creating an effective booking system for private events, showcasing the mobile nature of the business, and integrating with local community events. The project highlighted the importance of location-based services and the value of combining traditional craftsmanship with modern digital presence.

## Outcome

Firedough Pizza Strandhill successfully established a strong digital presence that supports the mobile pizzeria's operations. The website effectively communicates the authentic Italian heritage while highlighting the integration of local Irish ingredients. By providing easy booking and event information, the platform helps drive business growth and community engagement, allowing Firedough to bring authentic Neapolitan pizza to the west coast of Ireland.
